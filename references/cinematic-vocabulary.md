# Cinematic Vocabulary for Seedance Prompts

A categorized reference of visual descriptors proven to produce high-quality output with Seedance 2.0.
Copy-paste these terms directly into your prompts.

## Camera Movements

| Term | Effect | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| extreme close-up | Fills frame with a single detail | Hook shots, material texture reveals |
| close-up | Shows product at intimate distance | Feature demonstrations |
| medium shot | Product + surrounding context | In-use scenes |
| wide shot | Full environment visible | Lifestyle context, before/after |
| slow-motion | Dramatic time dilation | Product function demos, satisfying moments |
| camera slowly pulls back | Gradual reveal from detail to whole | Tech Reveal template hooks |
| camera orbits around | 360° product showcase | Premium product display |
| tracking shot | Camera follows motion | Product-in-use sequences |
| dolly zoom | Vertigo effect, background shifts | Dramatic price reveal moments |
| top-down overhead | Bird's eye flat lay | Product unboxing, arrangement |
| shallow depth of field | Blurred background, sharp subject | Isolating product from environment |
| rack focus | Focus shifts between foreground/background | Transition between two elements |

## Prompt Length Guide

| Element | Recommended Length |
|---------|-------------------|
| Full prompt | 150–300 words (sweet spot for Seedance 2.0) |
| Hook description | 2–3 sentences |
| Product demo | 3–5 sentences |
| Price/brand overlay | 2–3 sentences |
| CTA | 1–2 sentences |
| Safety zone + language rules | 2 fixed sentences (always include) |

**Too short** (<100 words): Seedance fills in blanks with generic content
**Too long** (>400 words): Model may ignore later instructions or produce incoherent results
**Sweet spot** (150–300 words): Enough detail for cinematic quality, short enough for consistent execution
